Introduction

Extreme heat weather has become more frequent in the United States in the recent decades. When people are exposed to extreme heat, they can suffer from potentially heat-related illnesses such as heat exhaustion and heat stroke. In the worst case, extreme weather can directly or indirectly increase the incidence of deaths. This website is designed to remember the lives lost due to this natural disaster and to alarm the public of the threat of extreme heat in the hope of calling for individual actions that stop climate change.
